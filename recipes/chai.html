Chai Recipe

Description

Magnificent milk-based tea crafted with spices from the subcontinent.

Ingredients
  • Milk
  • Black Tea
  • Ginger
  • Cinnamon Sticks
  • Cardamom
  • Cloves
  • Water
  • Brown Sugar
Steps
  1. Crush the spices
  2. Add crushed spices to water
  3. Boil milk in saucepan
  4. Add water and black tea to milk
  5. Leave to simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Add brown sugar and enjoy!
