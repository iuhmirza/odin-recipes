Tuna Sandwich Recipe

Description

A simple and pleasant cucumber and tuna sandwich.

Ingredients
  • White Bread
  • Mayo
  • Tuna
  • Cucumber
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Slice bread into preferred shape
  2. Slice cucumber
  3. Mix tuna and mayo (add salt and pepper to liking)
  4. Butter slices of bread
  5. Place cucumber slices on top of bread
  6. Spread tuna mixture across bread
  7. Join slices and enjoy!
